About Us

Our Story

Our story begins where great ideas often do: among friends. In 2024, a simple idea sparked among a group of high school students after a birthday party—an idea that would soon become SISU.

After months of brainstorming, testing, and refining, we launched our first product, a badminton bracelet, in February 2025, officially kicking off the SISU journey.

Since then, we've kept pushing forward. From badges and hair accessories to T-shirts and more, every product carries our energy and boldness, designed to move with you, on and off the court.

SISU is more than products. It's a mindset. It's about the courage to push your limits, the resilience to bounce back after setbacks, and the confidence to own your story.

With a growing community of passionate and engaged supporters, SISU continues to evolve, inspiring people everywhere to embrace their individuality, live boldly, and embody the spirit of Be brave, be you, be SISU every day.

Our Vision

To inspire a generation to move boldly, express themselves freely, and embrace life with courage, resilience, and confidence.

SISU envisions a world where sport, attitude, and lifestyle seamlessly come together, empowering every individual to live authentically and boldly.

Our Values

SISU, a word from Finnish that embodies courage, resilience, and confidence.

These are the values at the heart of everything we do:

Courage

Dare to push limits and explore new possibilities.

Resilience

Embrace challenges, learn from setbacks, and keep moving forward.

Confidence

Own your story and express yourself authentically.
